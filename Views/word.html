• To force a table to the top or bottom of the page:
    1. Right click on table and select table properties
    2. Change text wrapping to "Around" and select the now available "Positioning"
    3. Change desired vertical positioning.
    4. To adjust Header/footer margins: Go to Header/Footer Design tab, In the Position Section, change"Header/Footer from Top"
Word 2003 (7) To fix an oversized header, go to Page Layout->Margins->Custom Margains->Layout->Change "Verticle Alignment" to Top
